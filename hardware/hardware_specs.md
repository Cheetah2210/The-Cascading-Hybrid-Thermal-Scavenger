# CHTS Physical Specifications

This file contains the absolute dimensions 
for building the hardware components.

## 1. The Expansion Nozzle
* Type: Convergent-divergent style
* Inlet Diameter: 6.0 mm
* Throat Orifice: 1.5 mm
* Outlet Width: 20.0 mm

## 2. The Solid-State MHD Channel
* Total Channel Length: 100.0 mm
* Internal Width: 20.0 mm
* Internal Height: 5.0 mm
* Wall Thickness: 3.0 mm

## 3. Magnet Pocket Dimensions
* Placement: Left and right exterior walls
* Length: 50.0 mm
* Height: 20.0 mm
* Depth: 10.0 mm
* Designed for: N52 Neodymium blocks

## 4. Power Electrodes
* Material: Copper or Gold-plated brass
* Strips: 2 parallel plates
* Length: 50.0 mm
* Location: Top and bottom internal surfaces
